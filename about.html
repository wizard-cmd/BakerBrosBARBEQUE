About

Baker Bros Barbeque

Baker Bros was started by Drew Baker, whose family have been in the business for the longest time. Right before baker bros have ever been a thing, when it was known as West Alley BBQ and Smokehouse, Drew's grandfather had owned a pharmacy right across from it. When west alley expanded out of downtown, Mr. Drew Baker Swoop right in there and seized the land in under 24 hours.

Bakers life Baker is what we call a “Jacksonian” Dedicating himself to his city. He’s family was always apart of downtown, his dad's Auto store and the as for mentioned drugstore. Baker believes this is he’s time to shine, add a little more to the city and people who raised him. Ever since he aquired his own store, he's affected downtown in many good ways. He transformed his restaurant into a area where culture may blossom.
Our Philosophy He isn’t just all words, Baker had worked in the food and beverage business for almost 35 years and even in west alley for 2 years. He has his own philosophy when it comes to grilling.
"My philosophy is let the pork speak for itself," Baker said. "We don't need to go crazy with sauce or go crazy with the rubs, we can just add things like that on the back end to accentuate the taste and not let those things dominate the taste. "I think pure West Tennessee style can speak for itself. We aren't Memphis, we aren't North Carolina, we aren't Kansas City and we aren't Texas. We have our own way of doing barbecue."
