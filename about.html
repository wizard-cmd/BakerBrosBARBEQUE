About

Baker Bros Barbeque

Baker Bros was started by Drew Baker, whose family has been in the Downtown area for a long time. Long before Baker Bros moved in, Drew's grandfather opened a pharmacy right across from the restaurant. When West Alley expanded out of downtown, Mr. Drew Baker swooped right in there and seized the location in under 24 hours.

Bakers life


Baker is what we call a “Jacksonian”, dedicating himself to his city. His family has always been a part of downtown, his dad's auto store and his grandfather's drugstore. Baker believes that this is his time to shine and add a little more to the city. Ever since he acquired his own store, he has affected downtown in many good ways. He transformed his restaurant into an area where culture may blossom.


Our Philosophy


He isn’t just all words, Baker has worked in the food and beverage business for almost 35 years, and he even worked in West Alley for 2 years. He has his own philosophy when it comes to grilling.



"My philosophy is let the pork speak for itself," Baker said. "We don't need to go crazy with sauce or go crazy with the rubs, we can just add things like that on the back end to accentuate the taste and not let those things dominate the taste. "I think pure West Tennessee style can speak for itself. We aren't Memphis, we aren't North Carolina, we aren't Kansas City and we aren't Texas. We have our own way of doing barbecue."
